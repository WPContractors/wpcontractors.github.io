Since 2019, WP Contractors has been helping agencies, government, and corporate clients source the best WordPress development and management resources worldwide.

Who We Are

WP Contractors is a self-funded start-up based in the Seattle area. The company began as a service of Celebrate Drupal, a U.S. based global Drupal consulting firm founded by Chris McGrath. Check out some of our past reviews on Clutch.

The company has 200 WP Contractors online and growing, as well as partner managers in Washington D.C., Chicago, San Francisco, New York, Boston, Atlanta, Denver, Austin, St. Louis.

We search the world every day, building relationships with the best WordPress talent.
